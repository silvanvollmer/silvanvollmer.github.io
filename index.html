Hi there, I am

Silvan Vollmer.

I am a PhD Candidate in Statistics at the Copenhagen Causality Lab of the University of Copenhagen under the supervision of Sebastian Weichwald and Niklas Pfister.

I am very much interested in causal inference methods and their application to real-world data. I am working on identifying and estimating causal effects under unobserved confounding using proxy variables. If this topic interests you, feel free to reach out to me :)
